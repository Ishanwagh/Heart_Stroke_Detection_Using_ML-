About Us

Our Mission

As heart diseases and strokes are increasing rapidly across the world and causing deaths, it becomes necessary to develop an efficient system that would predict the heart stroke effectively before hand so that immediate medical attention can be given.

Our Aim

To save life of people by predicting possiblity of heart stroke using various parameters thus a person can take have precatiouns.Machine Learning can play an essential role in predicting presence/absence of Heart Stroke. Such information, if predicted well in advance, can provide important insights to doctors who can then adapt their diagnosis and treatment per patient basis
